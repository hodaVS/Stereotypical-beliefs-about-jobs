# Stereotypical-beliefs-about-jobs
---
The task of comparing stereotypical beliefs about jobs among women and men

In this assignment, a list of jobs will be shown randomly to the individual and 
the subject will be asked to specify the degree of masculinity or femininity of 
each job on a continuum using mouse movement.
Also, if the job is consider to be genderless, the mouse should be placed in the middle of the continuum.
